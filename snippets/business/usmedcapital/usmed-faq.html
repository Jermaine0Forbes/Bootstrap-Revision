How long does it take to get my money?
Funding can take up to 24 hours to 3 business days.
Is there any cost to apply?
No cost / no obligation quote.
What can I use the money for?
Equipment purchases, bulk supply purchases, advertising / marketing, office renovation/remodeling, acquisitions of another practice and buy out of a partner, anything related to your business.
What do I need to submit for qualification?
Completed and signed application and four most recent months of business bank statements.
How much does it cost?
Rates range from 5%-45% of the amount of the loan or advance.
